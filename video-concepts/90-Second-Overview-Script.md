# Gemynd 90-Second Overview Video Script

**Target Length:** 90 seconds
**Tone:** Emotional but professional. Dignified. Trustworthy.

**00:00 - 00:15 (Hook)**
Visual: Warm, intimate shot of an elderly woman gently touching old photographs spread across a table.
Voiceover (warm female): "We take thousands of photos. We have lifetimes of stories. But most of them... disappear."

**00:15 - 00:35 (Problem)**
Visual: Quick cuts of boxes in attics, forgotten hard drives, blurry phone photos.
Voiceover: "Generic AI can generate a story in seconds. But when it matters most — when a family is grieving, or a legacy is being passed down — 'close enough' isn't good enough."

**00:35 - 01:05 (Solution + Proof)**
Visual: Epson scanner feeding documents. Voice note with Connie. Then the beautiful Source Panel showing citations from FamilySearch, photos, and spoken words.
Voiceover: "Gemynd is different. Every memory is grounded. Every claim has a source. From a box of photos or a single conversation to a cinematic legacy with full provenance — in minutes, not months."

**01:05 - 01:30 (Emotional Close)**
Visual: Family watching the finished cinematic video together. QR code being scanned at a funeral service.
Voiceover: "Because the stories that matter most deserve to be remembered exactly as they were lived."

**End Card:** Gemynd logo + "Grounded Memory Platform" + "Preserving what matters. Grounded in truth. Powered by AI." + website
