**Gemynd Brand & Strategy 2026**

Public working repository for Gemynd's brand system, marketing assets, and strategic planning.

## Positioning Overview

Gemynd operates with a deliberate **dual identity**:

- **WestFlow** = The Cognitive Orchestration Layer (platform + AI Swizzle methodology). Focused on document-to-agent workflows for copier dealers, SMBs, field service, and HR.
- **Gemynd** = The Grounded Memory Platform (human legacy & memory vertical, powered by WestFlow). Flagship product: StoryScribe.

**Core narrative:** "WestFlow turns documents and conversations into reliable, grounded agents. Gemynd applies that same provenance-first engine to the stories and memories that define who we are."

This structure allows us to speak credibly to technical/channel buyers while delivering the emotional, trust-based experience that funeral professionals, senior living communities, and families need.
